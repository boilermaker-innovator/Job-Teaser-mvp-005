Job Teaser Creator

Welcome to the Job Teaser Creator. This tool helps you prepare job information for AI-assisted teaser creation. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter the job ad URL and company logo URL (if available)
  2. Paste the full job ad into the form
  3. Review and edit the Job Teaser Template if needed
  4. Click "Prepare for AI" to format the content
  5. Use the "Copy to Clipboard" button to copy the prepared content
  6. Click on the ChatGPT or Claude button to open your preferred AI tool
  7. Paste the copied content into the AI chat to generate your job teaser

Pro Tip: The AI will enhance the provided template to create an interactive job teaser with tabs and animations.
